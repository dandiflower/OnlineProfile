About Me

Having grown up in Montana on a small llama ranch, I'm accustom to free spirited and eccentric approaches to problems. I'm an abstract thinker at heart, and I enjoy the process of tackling complex real life challenges through the use of science, technology, art and design.

I'm versed in a variety of 3D digital software and programming languages. So far I've had the priveledge to work in a variety of disciplines including art, film & television, architecture and digital fabrication, and tech.

In my free time I like to listen to British girl punk preferably while jogging through the San Francisco Bay Area's magestic redwood trees.
